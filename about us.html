About Us



PlanIt4U is a online event planning tool that transforms the event-planning and event-management industry by making everything you will ever need to think of, just a click away. We offer value to all our clients both vendors and hosts by giving everyone the chance to put your best foot forward. In essence we are an open marketplace where all our clients can meet and conduct business directly, from the comfort of their fingertips.


The most significance this is that when the client enters the budget which he wish to spend on his event and his requirements, website itself filters and creates a listing which suits his budget and the requirements. But they can customise anything going beyond the list if needed.
The PlanIt4U team is composed of a diverse group of talented, resourceful and ingenious individuals who are at the forefront of their specific fields which range from programming, QA, to management, marketing, and event-planning. This wide array of expertise is united to bring you the best service possible. Each of our clients can rest assured that we will converge our skill to ensure you have tailor-made digital solutions to suit your specific need and your events are as perfect as you dreamed them up to be. We are also dedicated to molding the next generation of world leaders in their chosen field and make a passion of it.

PlanIt4U was developed by the a team consists of 3 undergraduates of Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology. We strive to introduce innovative, global, and futuristic digital solutions to newer industries. Our team members are highly skilled free-thinkers who come from a wide diversity of backgrounds. We have an “infectious” enthusiasm to dream, innovate, lead, imagine, explore, understand, create, perform. If it’s high-tech, cutting edge, and if it adds value to your life, you will find us there!
